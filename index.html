About Us

Peniel Agri-Solutions (PAS) Ltd is a private company in Zambia, registered with PACRA. Founded in memory of its visionary director, PAS aims to enhance community well-being and was officially established in 2023. With over 10 years of experience, the founders focus on supporting small-scale farmers in rural Zambia through tailored technological solutions, addressing issues like low productivity and high poverty rates.

Our Mission

Peniel Agri Solutions is dedicated to delivering market-based solutions with mentorship and support. We focus on empowering women and youth in agriculture, helping them overcome challenges and achieve sustainable development. Our mission is to elevate farmers, drive transformative change, and economically uplift communities.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a more inclusive and equitable agricultural landscape. By harnessing Zambia's abundant natural resources, we strive to support farmers in achieving sustainable growth and development. Peniel Agri Solutions aims to be a catalyst for positive change in rural communities, driving progress and prosperity for all.

Company Values

With a particular emphasis on women and youth, Peniel Agri Solutions is committed to enhancing the livelihoods of smallholder families. The focus is on increasing productivity and elevating production levels, aiming to attract commercial interest from other market players. This is achieved through the adoption and utilization of improved and higher-yielding open varieties of legumes and cereals, coupled with the promotion of good farming and post-harvest management practices.

Empowering Women and the Youth

Empowering women and youth in agricultural production not only contributes to their individual economic independence but also plays a crucial role in enhancing food security, promoting sustainable farming practices, and driving overall rural development.

Empowering women in agriculture not only contributes to their individual well-being and economic independence but also enhances the overall resilience and sustainability of agricultural practices. It plays a crucial role in achieving broader social and economic development goals.

Cultivating Equality

Peniel Agri Solutions encapsulates the essence of promoting fairness and reaping the benefits of shared success. We want to foster equality in agricultural practices and, in doing so, harvest the rewards of prosperity for all involved. We want to empower women and youth, in the agricultural sector to achieve sustainable and equitable growth.

Procurement Services

We facilitate the acquisition of farmers' produce, streamlining the process and ensuring fair returns for their efforts.

Seed Production and Distribution

We specialize in the production and distribution of high-quality seeds, encompassing a variety of crops such as soybeans, groundnuts, cowpeas, sunflower, and sorghum. Our focus is on delivering seeds that meet stringent quality standards, fostering improved crop yields.

Technical Advisory Services

Our team of experts offers technical advisory services covering smart agricultural practices, sustainable farming techniques, and insights into farming as a business. We aim to empower farmers with knowledge that enhances their overall agricultural productivity and efficiency.

Financial Literacy

Recognizing the importance of financial management in agriculture, we provide financial literacy programs to equip farmers with the necessary skills to manage their resources effectively and make informed financial decisions.

Guaranteed Buy-Back Schemes

PAS is dedicated to fostering fair remuneration and improving market access for farmers through the implementation of guaranteed buy-back schemes and out-grower programs. Through these initiatives, PAS aims to create a more equitable and efficient agricultural ecosystem. By addressing challenges faced by farmers and ensuring fair compensation, we contribute to the economic upliftment of farming communities while fostering a sustainable and mutually beneficial relationship between the agricultural sector and our partners.
