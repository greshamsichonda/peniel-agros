About Us

Peniel Agri-Solutions (PAS) Ltd is a private company in Zambia, registered with PACRA. Founded in memory of its visionary director, PAS aims to enhance community well-being and was officially established in 2023.

With over 10 years of experience, the founders focus on supporting small-scale farmers in rural Zambia through tailored technological solutions, addressing issues like low productivity and high poverty rates.

Our Mission

flag

Empowering Farmers

Peniel Agri Solutions is dedicated to delivering market-based solutions with mentorship and support. We focus on empowering women and youth in agriculture, helping them overcome challenges and achieve sustainable development.

Our mission is to elevate farmers, drive transformative change, and economically uplift communities through innovative agricultural solutions.

Our Vision

A Transformed Agricultural Landscape

Our vision is to create a more inclusive and equitable agricultural landscape. By harnessing Zambia's abundant natural resources, we strive to support farmers in achieving sustainable growth and development.

Transforming Zambia's Agriculture

Peniel Agri Solutions stands as a beacon of progress in Zambia's agricultural sector, spearheading inclusive growth and empowerment. With a mission focused on empowering rural women and youth, and a visionary outlook for transforming the rural agribusiness landscape, PAS is positioned to make a substantial and enduring impact on Zambia's rural communities.

Our commitment to empowering women and youth is reflected in our initiatives that provide access to resources, education, and market opportunities. By fostering sustainable agricultural practices, PAS aims to not only enhance productivity but also contribute to the overall economic development of rural areas.

In essence, Peniel Agri Solutions is not just a participant in Zambia's agricultural sector; it is a catalyst for positive change, envisioning a future where rural communities are empowered, agribusiness flourishes, and prosperity becomes a tangible reality.
